Do you have access to your online retirement/annuity account?

Yes - Click on the button and input your credentials for your retirement account.
No - Follow the step-by-step instructions below.

  1. Call the Office of Personnel Management (OPM) at 1-800-767- 6738 and set up the password for your OPM Retirement Services Online Account.
  2. Have the new password sent to your email.
  3. Confirm you have access to your OPM Retirement Services Online account with your new password by clicking servicesonline.opm.gov (you will need access to this account later in the application).
  4. Continue with your loan application.
  • Call MyPay/DFAS at 1-888-332-7411 and set up the password for your online retirement/annuity account.
  • Have the new password sent to your email.
  • After you’ve set up your online account. Access your MyPay/DFAS online retirement/annuity account with your new password by clicking mypay.dfas.mil/#/ (you will need access to this account later in the application).
  • Continue with your loan application.
